Center for Adult Learning and Continuing Professional Education


In S.Y. 2018-2019, SPUP launched the Center for Adult Learning and Continuing Professional Education (CALCPE). It aims to:




1. offer courses that can be accredited by the Professional Regulations Commission on license renewal of professionals;
2. open opportunities for professional and career development to certain groups of individuals; and

3. assist adult learners in acquiring new competencies and lifelong learning skills.


SPUP was granted by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) as Continuing Professional Development (CPD) Provider.
The Certificate Courses may lead to Diploma Program. Diploma Courses may be credited in the Bachelor’s degree Program. Classes will be on blended mode considering the nature of work of enrollees, with at least 70% classroom contact either during evenings or weekends. Extension classes within Region 2 may be carried out depending on the number of enrollees from a certain place.
English courses at the SPUP Intercultural Institute for Languages may be considered under the Certificate in Foreign Language for international students. Indonesian graduates of Diploma in Midwifery may enroll in BS Midwifery under the transnational education program of SPUP.
